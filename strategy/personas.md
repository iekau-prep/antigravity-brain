# 🇯🇵 Target Audience: Japanese First-Time Home Buyers

To build effective tools and content, we must deeply understand our users.

## Persona 1: The Cautious Planner (Kenta & Yuki)
**"We want to stop paying rent, but a 35-year loan feels like a trap."**

-   **Profile:** 32-year-old couple, one child (2yo), potentially another on the way.
-   **Income:** Combined ¥9M/year (stable corporate jobs).
-   **Current Living:** Renting a 1LDK/2LDK in Tokyo/Yokohama suburbs (¥140k/month).
-   **Pain Points:**
    -   Overwhelmed by mortgage options (Flat 35 vs. Variable).
    -   Terrified of value depreciation (property value dropping by half in 20 years).
    -   Confused by "hidden costs" (brokerage fees, registration tax, renovation).
-   **Goal:** Buy a 3LDK condo (mansion) or small detachable house within 1 hour of commute. Budget: ¥50M - ¥60M.

## Persona 2: The Modern Single Professional (Haruto/Sakura)
**"Asset building is my priority. I need a home that is also an investment."**

-   **Profile:** 29-year-old single professional in IT/Finance.
-   **Income:** ¥7M - ¥10M/year.
-   **Current Living:** Renting a 1K/1LDK in central Tokyo (¥120k/month).
-   **Pain Points:**
    -   Will I be able to sell this if I get married or move?
    -   Is it better to buy a compact condo now or wait?
    -   Tax benefits (mortgage tax deduction) optimization.
-   **Goal:** Buy a 1LDK/2LDK in a high-demand area (Minato, Chuo, Shibuya) with good resale value. Budget: ¥45M - ¥70M.

## Key Anxiety Triggers (Japan Specific)
1.  **Earthquakes:** Is this building safe? (Shin-taishin vs. Kyu-taishin).
2.  **Depreciation:** Will my home be worthless in 30 years?
3.  **Variable Rates:** Interest rates are historically low (0.3% - 0.5%), but what if they rise?
